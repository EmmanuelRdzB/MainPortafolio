Who am I?

Hi. I am Emmanuel Rodriguez, but you can also call me by my nickname: Meme. I am 24 years old and I studied Computer Systems Engineering at the University of Guanajuato. I am currently in my process of graduation in addition to continuing with courses and personal projects that are of my interest.

Throughout my career, I had the opportunity to participate in school and extracurricular activities and projects that brought me closer to the idea of being able to become a video game developer. This idea came to fruition when I participate in GameCoder Academy, a curse given by the company GameCoder.

If you are interested in knowing a little more about my skills and experience, feel free to download my resume by clicking the button bellow, also you can check out W&E

Work & Experience

GameCoder Academy

I had the opportunity to participate in the GameCoder Academy courses. Being a quite long course (February 2020 - December 2020), I learned from the most basic topics to advance ones about Unreal Engine, using blueprints and also c++ programming.

Here are some games I worked on during this course.

Super Star Violet

Thanks to this first project i learned more about working with a multidisciplinary team, because we were divided into artists and programmers to bring Super Star Violet to life. My work in this project was the creation of an artificial intelligence for the enemies, as well as their collitions and programming.

Here are some pictures, but you can check out the game on itch.io

  • Go to game

LAByrinth

For this new project, I was chosen as team leader. I was in charge of the organization of the team, from how the meetings would be held to how we would do our work sessions. In addition, I was in the development of the GDD, as well as the level design, the main character programming and the interaction with objects, even taking care of my teammates to make sure they didn't have any problems during the development of the game.

Here are some pictures of the game, but you can check out more and download it at itch.io

  • Go to game

Mr. Weenie's Odyssey

Bringing Mr. Weenie's Odyssey to life turned out to be a great challenge, as we had to develop this game in 48 hours for the GMTK GameJam. My participation in this game was to help in the creation of the concept of the game, as well as the programming of the various mechanics, like player collitions, prop attachments and joints, character's faces and item collection.

I bealive this is one great game we made, I strongly recommend you go check it out.

Version Control

In all previous projects I learned how to use version control software. The software we used was Tortoisehg by Mercurial, but in general I understand how this kind of software, like git, works in order to do version control.
